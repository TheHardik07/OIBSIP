Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931 – 2015 | Scientist • President • Visionary

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”

Biography

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the “Missile Man of India,” he played a pivotal role in India’s space and defense programs. Beyond science, he inspired millions with his humility, vision, and dedication to education and youth empowerment.

Life & Achievements

  • 1931: Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1969: Joined ISRO and led SLV-III
  • 1998: Key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • 2002–2007: 11th President of India
  • 2015: Passed away while teaching students
